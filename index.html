SAVE MAX FROM IMPENDING DOOM!

Max is in trouble! After putting in some extra hours on his last space walk, Max was cut loose from his ship and is now left to drift aimlessly through space. Keep Max from running out of air and grab some oxygen tanks!

Features

Dodge and thrust your way throughout a endless void of space-junk. Compete with friends and survive the longest via competitive leaderboards. Scour space for Snax and earn bonus points!
